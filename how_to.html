To plan your day isn't a difficult task. For it, you should 1)Choose your habits and 2)Organize them

What habits should I choose?

Your habits should be chosen based on your goals.
Whether you want to have a productive day or a healthy lifestyle, you should define 1) What do I want? and 2) What should I do to get it?

How should I organize my day?

After deciding what is/are your goal(s), you should define When you are more productive (at morning? after lunch? evening?) and select the habits that will lead you to your main objectives to be done firstly.

Now go do it!
