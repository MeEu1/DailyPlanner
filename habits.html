Habits

Habits can be defined as 'rituals' and procceses we do automaticaly so our counsciousness can focus on more complex and immediate things, such as deciding what to eat. Common habits include brushing your teeth, surfing your social media when bored, taking showers, etc.

Health Habits

Health habits are the ones focused on your general well being.

Some examples of health habits are:

  • Going to the gym
  • Having a balanced diet
  • Running
  • Walking or cycling instead of using your car
  • Meditation

Productivity Habits

Productivity habits focus on your efficiency, on how productive your day will be.

Some examples of productivity habits are:

  • Journaling
  • Time management
  • Waking up early
  • Digital Minimalism
  • Sleeping 8 hours
  • Stop using your phone 1 hour before bed

Hobbies

Hobbies are habits based on leisure.

Some examples of hobbies are:

  • Hiking
  • Playing video games
  • Watching Streaming services such as Netflix and Youtube
  • Hanging out with friends
  • Learning a new language
  • Instagram
  • Github
